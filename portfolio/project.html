Multi Purpose Tracker

Description:

  • Users can record their daily tasks and log the quantities or progress in respective columns, making it easy to monitor their activities.
  • The application sends notifications and reminders to prompt users to complete their tasks, ensuring they stay on track with their daily responsibilities.
  • Users can share their tracking templates with others, fostering collaboration and enhancing productivity.

Purpose: This project aims to leverage the knowledge and experience I have gained throughout my three years of university studies and workplace engagements to develop a functional application. Additionally, I seek to explore and integrate new technologies, such as Firebase for backend services and Azure for cloud deployment, to further enhance my skills and adapt to industry standards.

Technologies/Tools

  • Java
  • Springboot
  • RESTful API
  • Azure
  • Firebase
  • Android Studio
