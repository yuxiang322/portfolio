Multi Purpose Tracker

Description:

  • Users can record their daily tasks and log the quantities or progress in respective columns, making it easy to monitor their activities.
  • The application sends notifications and reminders to prompt users to complete their tasks, ensuring they stay on track with their daily responsibilities.
  • Users can share their tracking templates with others, fostering collaboration and enhancing productivity.

Purpose: This project aims to leverage the knowledge and experience I have gained throughout my three years of university studies and workplace engagements to develop a functional application. Additionally, I seek to explore and integrate new technologies, such as Firebase for backend services and Azure for cloud deployment, to further enhance my skills and adapt to industry standards.

Technologies/Tools

  • Java
  • Springboot
  • RESTful API
  • Azure
  • Firebase
  • Android Studio

Interactive Web app

Description:

  • Web app for uploading of recipes
  • Users to be able to share their own special recipe/health meals for others to try out

Purpose: This food-sharing web and mobile app is designed for home cooks, food enthusiasts, and chefs to share their special recipes and healthy meals with a focused community. Key features include user profiles for uploading recipes and cooking tutorials, ingredient procurement, interaction through reviews and subscriptions, and monetization options via PayPal or credit/debit cards. A main feed showcases recipes categorized by cuisine, with search functionality for users, ingredients, and dish names. The app fosters a dedicated space for culinary sharing, discovery, and community engagement.

Technologies/Tools

  • React.js
  • TailwindCss
  • Springboot
